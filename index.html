ABOUT US

SawitCo was founded in 2022, based on awareness of the potential and promising prospect of the local palm-based products in Indonesia.

From our beginnings as a local palm-based manufacturer and producer, we built our business on excellence in product quality and services.

Our ambitious growth programs are grounded in a commitment to develop our core values and also contribute to the local economic growth in Indonesia.
